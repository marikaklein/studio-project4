Ginger Noodles

  • 12 oz bok choy
  • 1 oz ginger
  • 8 oz noodles
  • 2 tbsps peanut oil
  • 1/4 cup & 1 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsps rice vinegar
  • 1/2 cup sliced scallions
  • 3 finely chopped garlic cloves
  • 1 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsps sesame seeds
  1. cook bok choy tops and steams separately
  2. peel and finely chop 1/2 of ginger
  3. slice rest of ginger into matchsticks
  4. cook noodles according to directions
  5. heat 1 tbsp peanut oil in skillet & stir in 1/2 scallions, chopped ginger, and garlic. cook until fragrant
  6. add bok choy stems, cook for about 2 mins, add tops
  7. add noodles, 1/4 cup of soy sauce, & rice vinegar
  8. cook & garnish
Restart

Spanish Tortillas

  • 2 potatoes
  • 3-4 tbsps olive oil
  • 4-5 eggs
  1. thinly slice potatoes
  2. heat olive oil and cook until translucent
  3. break up as slices soften
  4. whisk eggs and finished potatoes together
  5. cook in pan again
  6. flip using a plate on top and serve

Broccoli Cheddar Soup

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 1/2 cups broccoli
  • 1 cup carrots
  • 1 thinly sliced celery stick
  • 2 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  1. melt butter in skillet over med/high heat
  2. saute onion in butter ~5 mins
  3. whisk melted butter & flour in large saucepan over low/med heat until flour loses granular texture
  4. gradually pour milk in and whisk constantly
  5. add chicken stock, simmer ~20 mins
  6. add vegetables, simmer another ~20 mins
  7. add cheese & serve once melted

Fettuccine Alfredo

  • 1 small onion, minced
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 stick of butter
  • 1/2 pack of cream cheese (4 oz)
  • 2 cups milk or heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup parm
  • 1/2 cup parsley
  • 1/4 cup pasta water
  • desired amount of fettucine
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  1. start cooking pasta
  2. add garlic & onion to heated pan with oil
  3. add butter & let melt
  4. add cream cheese, milk/heavy cream, parm, & parsley
  5. add spices & pasta water
  6. let sauce come to a boil then add pasta & serve

Crepes

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  1. whisk flour & eggs
  2. mix in milk & water
  3. add salt & butter
  4. mix until smooth
  5. heat lightly oiled pan over med heat
  6. cook ~2 mins each side
  7. brush with melted butter & serve
